About

Project Management Professional (PMP), SAFe 5.1 Program Consultant (SPC), Agile Coach and Certified ScrumMaster (CSM), and Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) with 20 years IT related experience showcasing deep knowledge and experience with applying Agile frameworks and practices within IT organizations, exceptional general management, project and program management, and systems engineering skills with strong background in highly regulated industries.

Coaches ScrumMasters, Product Owners, Agile Teams, and Others on Lean-Agile frameworks (SAFe, Scrum, Kanban, Scrumban, XP), Agile SDLC/QA, and DevOps. Works with and coaches Project / Program Managers, Technical Leads, Developers and Engineers in best practices based on Lean Waterfall, Agile, and Hybrid approaches with a goal of promoting more DevOps oriented teams.

Constantly drives process and performance improvement by identifying and tracking metrics to be measured during and after Agile sprints, releases and through the entire Agile SDLC as well as traditional or hybrid projects, adjusting as necessary, modifying or creating new processes and continually seeking out opportunities for continual improvement.

Coaches and works with executive leadership and mid-level management on organizational change management and agile transformation related initiatives.
